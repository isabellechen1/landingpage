Pixel Series

Google Pixel is a brand of consumer electronic devices developed by Google that run either Chrome OS or the Android operating system.
The Pixel brand was introduced in February 2013 with the first-generation Chromebook Pixel.
The Pixel line includes laptops, tablets, and smartphones, as well as several accessories.
